# Module 01 — Introduction to Neuroscience

This module introduced the foundational question driving the entire course: what can the biological brain teach us about building better AI? Topics covered the basic organization of the nervous system, how neurons communicate, and the historical relationship between neuroscience and computing.

## Key Concepts
- Biological vs. artificial intelligence — complementarity vs. competition
- Neural computation basics: dendrites, soma, axon, synapses
- Energy efficiency of the brain (~20W) vs. modern AI systems
- Predictive processing and world models
